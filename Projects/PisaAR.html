Pisa AR

Augmented Reality

Unity - Vuforia

This project is created for Pisa Laboratories to controll some of their internal process. When pointing the camera at a QR code, that it will be pasted on the machines, it will show a exact 3D model of a internal machine system, that way the operator can see how it is, how it works and if he needs to replace pieces or see more information about that system, click on one of the respective buttons that would redirect you to a website of the exact system you were viewing, where you would see all that information.
