Beblok

Augmented Reality

Unity - EasyAR

Beblok is an AR project created for a company that creates different kind of cardboard protectors. The project shows to a Beblok client how the cardboards works in different kind of situations. Works by pointing the phone camera to the image target and it will activate the 3D models and the UI to control the experience and see the different beblok products. You can rotate and scale the 3D model to see it better.
