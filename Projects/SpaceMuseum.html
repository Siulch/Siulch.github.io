Space Museum

Virtual Reality

Unity - Cardboard

Project that simulates a space museum, the museum is about the future of Virtual Reality on Space subjects. You move by clicking on the spheres at the center and in every station you can read some info about that subject, see a video or see some images there's always a voice that's reading all the info. When clicking in the video of the purple station it activates a 360 video that reproduces inside the game created with unity.
